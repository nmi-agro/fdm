Add responsive mobile view for the Timeline page. On viewports narrower than 768px, and on landscape viewports up to 1024px wide, the Gantt chart is replaced by a date-grouped event feed with sticky headers, a sticky active-cultivations bar, month/year filtering, a Datum/Veld toggle, collapsible field-grouped view, infinite scroll, and empty states.
