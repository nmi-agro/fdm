Organizations can now see an overview of the BLN3 measures and indicators for the farms they have access to. They can view average indicators for each farm together on a heatmap atlas, and quickly navigate to a farm's own atlas by clicking on one of its fields. On the new organization indicators page they can see the hierarchy of indicator scores and see the top 5 offending farms, just like how they are able to see top 5 offending fields in the corresponding farm page. On the new organization measures page they can see all farm measures, and quickly navigate to the measures overview page of a farm that has a measure in one of its fields.
